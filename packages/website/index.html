BPMN Engines Are Powerful. But Isolated.

BPMN engines like Camunda 7, Operaton, and CIBSeven excel at workflow orchestration. They're stable, proven, battle-tested.

The challenge? They don't talk to the outside world without custom code. Need to send a Slack message? Write code. Post to an API? More code. Connect to your CRM? Even more code.

Integration platforms like n8n have thousands of pre-built connectors. But they're disconnected from your BPMN workflows.

What if you could have both?
